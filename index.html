About Norton Gold Fields: Norton Gold Fields is one of Australia's largest domestic gold producers, with recent annual production of up to 200,000 ounces. The Company has a mining and processing complex in Western Australia's world-class Kalgoorlie goldfield region, including a highly prospective tenement package of more than 1,000km¬≤ surrounding the 3.8Mtpa Paddington Mill.

About this role:

The Mechanical Leading Hand is responsible for assisting the Mechanical Supervisor by providing day to day leadership to the mechanical maintenance team. You will also be required to assist in installation, maintenance and repair of all mechanical systems and components at Paddington Operations' fixed plant (Processing Mill) and its surrounding work sites.

Key Duties:

  • Undertake preventative maintenance inspections, equipment repairs and associated component change outs.
  • Relief coverage for Mechanical Supervisor, where required.
  • Diagnosis of faults and breakdown
  • Carry out time and material estimations for planned tasks and enter associated parts orders.
  • Ensure sufficient task analysis are completed to carry out work safely and in a timely manner.
  • Coordinate all allocated work to trade employees in accordance with directions.
  • Demonstrate Safety Leadership, proactively participate in incident investigations
  • Provide training, supervision and support to Apprentices, where required.

Qualifications and Experience:

  • Cert III in Engineering - Mechanical Trade (Must-have)
  • Current unencumbered C Class Western Australian Drivers Licence
  • Minimum of 2 years mine site fixed plant mechanical experience
  • Minimum of 5 years post apprentice mechanical work experience
  • Senior First Aid (desirable)
  • Front Line Management Certificate IV (desirable)
  • Forklift, EWP & CN Crane tickets (desirable)
  • HR ticket (desirable)

Benefits:

  • Salary + Performance Bonus + Residential Allowance
  • Insurance + Private Health Cover
  • Gym Membership Discount
  • Comprehensive health and wellbeing programs
  • EAP support
  • Family friendly roster
